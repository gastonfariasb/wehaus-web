WeHaus




Home Automation made simple


Remote Control

Brings a built-in infrared remote which enables you to control any device within reach.

Motion Sensor

Detects movement in front of the WeHaus, feeding this information to you and the system, let the magic happen.

Light Notifications

With its RGB LED you can configure it to let you know about the state of the virtual and real world.

Power Switches

One of its most powerful features. Connect any device to this power source and start controling it remotely. Popular choices include lights.
